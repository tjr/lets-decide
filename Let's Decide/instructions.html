You can use this application to arrive at a decision between multiple options, such as selecting a movie to watch or what to have for dinner. All you have to do is:

  1. Enter the options to decide between.
  2. Enter the names of the people voting.
  3. For each person, arrange the options in their preferred order.

The application can then display the result of combining everyone's preferences.

Usage Hints

  • Swipe an option or voter to delete it.
  • Shake to delete all options or voters.
  • The results screen shows the tally of all options in order from most votes to least, along with the number of points awarded each option using a borda count preference schedule algorithm.
